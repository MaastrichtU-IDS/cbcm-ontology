Label

Company form

Description

Name given to describe the structure of a corporation or business in the different legal orders. For example Limited (Ltd.), Public Limited Company (PLC), Gesellschaft mit beschrnkter Haftung (GmbH), Aktiengesellschaft (AG). The company legal form is regulated under national law and grants specific rights and obligations to the company stakeholders (such as shareholders, management & employees). - This is an entity positioning annotation generated by CoModIDE (https://comodide.com/).
