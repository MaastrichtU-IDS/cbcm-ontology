Label

Cross-border merger

Description

A cross border merger involves at least one company based in an EU Member State and one company that is registered elsewhere within the European Union and is leading to a universal transfer of assets and liabilities from the merging company to the acquiring company. - This is an entity positioning annotation generated by CoModIDE (https://comodide.com/).
