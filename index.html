The EU Cross-border Company Mobility (EU-CBCM) Ontology

The EU Cross-border Company Mobility (EU-CBCM) Ontology is an application ontology developed using the Web Ontology Language (OWL) to cover relevant terminology from EU Law related to cross-border company movement within the EU cbcm-ontology
